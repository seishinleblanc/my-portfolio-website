ReManga

🌐 View Live App

Overview

ReManga is a collector’s tool for tracking owned manga volumes, complete with shelf organization and smart tagging features.

Technologies Used

  • Swift & SwiftUI
  • Firebase Firestore & Authentication
  • Apple Store integration
  • Custom badge system

Why I Built It

I wanted a clean, intuitive app to help manga fans log their physical collections—something I couldn’t find with existing tools.

Challenges

Designing around Firestore’s structure was tricky—especially syncing up-to-date manga data across multiple sources. Also ensuring a clean, dark-mode friendly UI while preserving hierarchy required iterative testing.
